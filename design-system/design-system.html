TWO LINES OF LIGHT · DARK BODY · NOTHING ELSE

The story

Pun + universe. Two planets, one orbit. The dark Earth body is lit by exactly two lines: the sun-rim on the day-side limb, and a teal aurora on the night-side. Inspired by the Artemis 2 lunar flyby photographs.

The bowl of the P is a literal orbit. The Earth inside is the system. The aurora is the agent — alive, atmospheric, alert.

§07 — Rules

Do · Don't

Do

  • ✓Wrap main content in max-w-6xl mx-auto px-6 pb-16.
  • ✓Use <GlassCard> for any grid item, tier, metric.
  • ✓Use <GlowButton> for ALL primary CTAs.
  • ✓Wrap hero text + grids in <Reveal> / <StaggerReveal>.
  • ✓Append a lucide icon to button interiors with gap-2.
  • ✓Reserve font-display italic for hero / signature italics only.
  • ✓Keep body copy at text-gray-400 — pure white is reserved for headers.

Don't

  • ✗Inject px-* / py-* / text-lg into a GlowButton's className — it breaks the gradient border alignment.
  • ✗Apply pure text-white to body paragraphs.
  • ✗Build raw HTML cards instead of using <GlassCard>.
  • ✗Use light-mode backgrounds. There is no light mode.
  • ✗Add saturated solid-color backgrounds. Color comes from blurred orbs and gradient borders only.
  • ✗Stack multiple gradients on a single page region — one tri-stop is the rule.
  • ✗Recolor the wordmark or the logo lockup.
